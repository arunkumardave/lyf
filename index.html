Why Lyf exists
The healthiest people never go to the gym
Village farmers walk 10km a day — not on a treadmill, but through living. They eat with the seasons, sleep with the sun, and belong to a community. Modern health apps missed all of that. Lyf brings those principles back, personalised by AI, built for today's urban life.

AI that actually knows you
Powered by Gemini (free) or Claude (premium). Generates personalised meal plans, workouts, book passages, and nature suggestions — based on your stats, mood, and goals.
📊
Built on real data
Upload your InBody report and Lyf reads your body fat, muscle mass, visceral fat, and waist-hip ratio — then builds your plan around what the numbers actually say.
🔒
Private by design
Your data stays yours. No selling, no ads, no third-party sharing. Lyf is a tool, not a platform trying to own your attention.
