Why Lyf exists
Most health apps obsess over calories and steps. Lyf was built on a different belief — that the healthiest people in the world aren't gym-obsessed. They're village farmers who walk naturally, eat seasonally, sleep with the sun, and live in community. Lyf brings that philosophy to modern urban life, with AI to personalise every aspect of it for you.
